Built by one person, for everyone

Civic Nexus is an independent project, no VC funding, no ad revenue, no political sponsors. The tools are free to use because public understanding of politics shouldn't cost anything. Your support goes directly to the developer to cover hosting, research time, and continued development of new tools like the Values Survey, Social Perception Index, and Policy Simulator.

Where your money goes
Hosting & Infrastructure Server costs, API hosting, and database upkeep to keep tools online and fast.
Development Time Building and refining new tools, Values Survey, Social Perception Index, and more.
Research & Accuracy Sourcing literature and philosophy texts to keep definitions grounded and unbiased.
Outreach & Availability Ensuring Civic Nexus stays accessible, discoverable, and free for everyone globally.
